śīlaskandhanirdeśaparivartaḥ |tasmāttarhi kumāra ya ākāṅkṣedbodhisattvo mahāsattvaḥ kimityahaṃ
sukhamanuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhimabhisaṃbudhyeyamiti, tena kumāra bodhisattvena mahāsattvena
śīlaskandhe supratiṣṭhitena bhavitavyam, sarvabodhisattveṣu ca śāstṛpremasaṃjñā upasthāpayitavyā
||atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ velāyāmimā gāthā abhāṣata-yaḥ śīlaskandhe pratiṣṭhitu
bodhisattvohitaiṣicitto vicarati cārikāyām |kṣipraṃ sa gatvā abhiratibuddhakṣetraṃkṣāntiṃṃ
labhitvā bhaviṣyati dharmarājaḥ

tasmāt samagrā bhavatha aduṣṭacittāḥsarve ca bhogā satata manāpakārī |dṛṣṭvā ca buddhān śirighana
aprameyānbodhiṃ spṛśitvā bhaviṣyatha dharmasvāmī

tasmācchruṇitvā ima vara ānuśaṃsāndṛṣṭā ca bhikṣūn parama suśīlavantā |niḥśāṭhiyeno vidu sada
sevitavyāḥsamādhiptāptā bhaviṣyatha nocireṇa

sacennidhānāparimitāpramāṇapūrṇā bhaveyu maṇiratanebhi saptaiḥ |tathaiva bhūyo ratanavarāṇa
pūrṇāḥkṣetrā bhaveyurvālikagaṅgatulyāḥ

dānādhimukto bhaviya sa bodhisattvaekaika rātriṃdivamiha dānu dadyāt |evaṃ dadan so bahuvidha
kalpakoṭīḥno viṣṭhitaḥ syād vālika gaṅgatulyāḥ

yaśco samādhiṃ imumiha bodhisattvośrutvāna dhāreta sugatavarāṇa gañjam |yaḥ puṇyaskandho bhavati
gṛhītu tenotat sarvadānaṃ kalamapi nānubhoti

eṣo varo anupama puṇyaskandhojñānasya kośa aparimitākaropama |śrāddho naro yo imu ānulomikaṃdhāreyya
agraṃ imu virajaṃ samādhim

dhāreyya eta viraja samādhi śāntamahādhano bhavati sa bodhisattvaḥ |mahāsamudro bahuvidharatanasya
ākarona tasya puṇyasya pramāṇamasti

varehi dharmehi acintiyehisaṃbṛhito vuccati bodhisattvaḥ |na tasya bodhāya kadāci saṃśayoya
uddiśeyāti imaḥ samādhim

saṃsthāpya lokācariyaṃ vināyakaṃbuddhaṃ mahākāruṇikaṃ svayaṃbhuvam |yaḥ puṇyaskandhena vareṇupetoacintiyo
yasya pramāṇu nāsti

na uttaro tasya ca sattva kaścitmahāsahasrāya kadāci vidyate |yaḥ puṇyaskandhena samo bhavetajñānena
vāsādṛśācintiyena

anyatra yaḥ śrutva samādhimetaṃdhāreyya vāceyya paryāpuṇeyyā |paryeṣamāṇo'tula budhabodhiṃna
tasya jñānena samo bhaveta

sacet kumārā siya ayu dharmarūpayaḥ puṇyaskandho upacitu tena bhoti |dhāratu vācetu imaṃ samādhiṃna
so viceyyā iha pṛthulokadhātuṣu

tasmāt kumāreha ya bodhisattvoākāṅkṣate pūjitu sarvabuddhān |asaṅganirdeśapadārthakovidoatīta
utpanna tathāgatāṃścadhāretu vācetu imaṃ samādhim

eṣā hi sā bodhi tathāgatānāṃśraddhehi mahyaṃ vacanaṃ kumārāḥ |na bhāṣate vācamṛṣāṃ tathāgatona
hīdṛśāḥ sattva mṛṣāṃ vadanti

yasmin mayā śodhitu ātmagrāhoitaḥ pure kalpaśatānacintiyān |śreṣṭhā carantena pi bodhicārikāṃparyeṣamāṇena
imāṃ samādhim

tasmādimaṃ śrutva atha dharmagañjaṃyaḥ sūtrakoṭīnayutāna āgamaḥ |yaḥ puṇyaskandho vipulo acintiyoyeno
laghuṃ budhyati buddhajñānam

sarveṣa sūtrāṇidamagrasūtra-macintiyasyo kuśalasya ākaram |paryantu dharmāṇa na teṣa labhyateyāṃ
so sadā nirdiśate viśāradaḥ

chinditva bhinditva mahāsahasraṃśakyaṃ gaṇetuṃ paramāṇusaṃcayaḥ |na tveva te sūtraśatā acintiyānpramātu
yaṃ bhāṣati so aviṣṭhitaḥ

āśvāsa praśvāsa gaṇetu śakyaṃsarveṣa sattvāniha buddhakṣetre |paryantu sūtrāṇa na teṣa śakyaṃyān
bhāṣate so'tra samādhiye sthitaḥ

buddhāna kṣetrā yatha gaṅgavālikāye teṣa sattvā gati teṣūpapannāḥ |gaṇetu te śakyamathāpi cintituṃna
teṣa sūtrāṇa ya nityu bhāṣate

gaṇetu śakyamita kalpakoṭibhiḥmahāsamudreṣviha yātti vālikāḥ |nadīṣu kuṇḍeṣu hradeṣu tadvadananta
sūtrānta sa yat prabhāṣate

śakyaṃ gaṇetuṃ bahukalpakoṭiṣuya āpaskandhaḥ sada tatra tiṣṭhati |śatāya bhinnāya vālāgrakoṭiyosvarāṅga
teṣāṃ na tu śakyu sarvaśaḥ

śakyaṃ gaṇetuṃ bahukalpakoṭibhi-rye sattva āsan purimeṇa tatra |ya ātmabhāve vinibaddhasārāna
teṣa sūtrāntanirhāra jānitum

gaṇetu śakyaṃ ruta sarvaprāṇināṃye santi sattvā daśasu diśāsu |na śakyu sūtrānta gaṇetu tasyayad
bhāṣate'sau satatamaviṣṭhitaḥ

sarveṣa dharmāṇa nideśu jānatiniruktinirdeśapadārthakovidaḥ |viniścaye bhūtanayeṣu śikṣitoviśālabuddhiḥ
sada harṣaprajñaḥ

abhinnabuddhirvipulārthacintīacintya cinteti sadā prajānati |ghoṣasvabhāvaṃ pṛthu sarva jānatīśabdāṃśca
tān nirdiśato na sajjati

asakta so vuccati dharmabhāṇakona sajjate sarvajagasya bhāṣataḥ |praśnāna nirdeśapadehi kovidaḥtathāhi
teno paramārthu jñātaḥ

ekasya sūtrasyupadeśakoṭiyoacintiyāṃ niordiśato na sajjati |asaṅganirdeśapadārthakovidobhāṣantu
so parṣagato na sajjate

yaḥ susthito bhoti iho samādhiyesa bodhisattvo bhavatī akampiyaḥ |dharme balādhānaviśeṣaprāptaḥkaroti
so'rthaṃ bahuprāṇakoṭinām

yathaiva meruracalo akampiyaḥsarvehi vātehi na śakya kampitum |tathaiva bhikṣurvidu dharmabhāṇakaṃkampetu
śakyaṃ na parapravādibhiḥ

mahāsahasreṣviha lokadhātuṣuye parvatā ukta akampanīyāḥ |te śakya vātena prakampanāyana tveva
dharme sthitu śūnyi bhikṣuḥ

ya śūnyatāyāṃ satataṃ prayuktobuddhāna eṣo niyataṃ vihāraḥ |prajānatī niścitu dharma śūnyāṃsa
sarvavādībhi na śakyu kṣobhitum

akampiyo bhoti parapravādibhiḥsavapravādehi anābhibhūtaḥ |anābhibhūtaśca aninditaścaimumuddiśitvāna
samādhi śāntam

gatiṃ gato bhoti sa śunyatāyāṃsarveṣu dharmeṣu na kāṅkṣate'sau |anantajñāne sada supratiṣṭhitoimumuddiśitvāna
samādhi śāntam

balāni bodhyaṅga na tasya durlabhāpratisaṃvido ṛddhividhī acintiyā |abhijña no tasya bhavanti
durlabhādhāretva vācetva ima samādhim

bhavābhivṛttasya na tasya durlabhaṃanantajñānena jināna darśanam |saṃbuddha koṭīnayutānacintiyānso
drakṣyate etu samādhi dhārayan

sarveṣa co teṣa jināna antikesa śroṣyate etu samādhi śāntam |vareṇa jñānena upetu bheṣyatīpratisaṃvidāsu
vaśa pāramiṃ gataḥ

saced bhavenmaṇiratanāna pūrṇāmahāsahasrā iya lokadhātuḥ |ye divya śreṣṭhā maṇiratanāḥ pradhānāheṣṭaṃ
upādāya bhavāgru yāvat

yāvanta kṣetrā bahuvidha te anantājāmbūnadāsaṃstṛta pūrṇa sarve |dānaṃ dade jinavareṣu sarvaṃbhūmītalādupari
bhavāgra yāvat

yāvanti santi bahu vividhā hi sattvādānaṃ dadeyurvividhamanantakalpān |buddhāna dadyuḥ satatamaviṣṭhihantobodhyarthiko
co daditu dānaskandham

yaścaiva bhikṣurabhiratu śūnyatāyāṃbuddhānnamasye daśanakhaprāñjalīyo |na sa dānaskandhaḥ purimaku
yāti saṃkhyāṃyaḥ śūnyatāyāmabhiratu bodhisattvaḥ

taṃ co labhitvā sa hi naru puṇyavantodānaṃ dadeti vipulu janetva śraddhām |paryeṣamāṇo atuliya
buddhabodhiṃaupamyametaṃ kṛtu puruṣottamena

yaśco samādhimimu varu śreṣṭha gṛhṇe-ccatuṣpadāṃ gātha sa tuṣṭacittaḥ |yaḥ puṇyaskandho upacitu
tena bhotitat sarvadānaṃ śatimakalā nu bhoti

na tāva śīghraṃ pratilabhi buddhajñānaṃdānaṃ dadet so hitakaru bodhisattvaḥ |aśrutva etaṃ viraju
samādhi śāntaṃyatha śrutva śīghraṃ labhati sa buddhajñānam

yaśco labhitvā imu vara śāntabhūmiṃśrutasya gotraṃ imu virajaṃ samādhim |puryāpuṇeyyā pramuditu
bodhisattvaḥsa śīghrametaṃ pratilabhi buddhajñānam

yo'pī nidhānaṃ pratilabhi evarūpaṃkṣetrānanantān yathariva gaṅgavālikāḥ |te co bhaveyurmaṇiratanāna
pūrṇādivyāna co tathapi ca mānuṣāṇām

durdharṣu so bhoti prebhūtakośomahādhano dhanaratanenupetaḥ |yo bodhisattvo labhati imaṃ samādhiṃparyāpuṇantaḥ
satatamatṛptu bhoti

rājyaṃ labhitvā paramasamṛddha sphītaṃna tena tuṣṭo bhavati kadāci vijñaḥ |yathā labhitvā imu
virajaṃ samādhiṃtuṣṭo udagro bhavati sa bodhisattvaḥ

te te dharmadharā bhavanti satataṃ buddhāna sarvajñināṃdhārentī varadharmanetri vipulāṃ kṣīṇāntakāle
tathā |dharmakośadharā mahāmatidharāḥ sarvajñagañjaṃdharāḥte te sattva sahasrakoṭiniyutāṃstoṣanti
dharmasvaraiḥ

te te śīladhanenupeta matimān śikṣādhanāḍhyā narāḥte te śīlavrate sthitā abhiratā dharmadrumasyāṅkurāḥ
|te te raktakaṣāyacīvaradharā naiṣkramyatuṣṭāḥ sadāte te sattvahitāya apratisamāḥ
sarvajñatāṃ prasthitāḥ

te te dānta sudānta sattvadamakā damathenupetāḥ sadāte te śānta suśāntatāmanugatāḥ śāntapraśāntendriyāḥ
|te te supta prasupta sattva satataṃ dharmasvanairbodhayībodhitvā varaśreṣṭha
dharmaratanaiḥ sattvān pratiṣṭhāpayī

te te dānapatī bhavanti satataṃ sada muktatyāgī vidute te matsariyairna saṃvasi mahātyāge ramante
sadā |te te sattva daridra dṛṣṭva dukhitān bhogehi saṃtarpayīte te sattvahite sukhāya
satataṃ sarvajñatāṃ prasthitāḥ

te te āhani dharmabheri vipulāṃ jñāne sadā śikṣitāḥchindantī jana sarva saṃśayalatāṃ jñāne sadā
prasthitāḥ |te te suśruta dharmadhāri virajā sūtrāntakoṭīśatānparṣāyāṃ sthita āsane matidharāḥ
pravyāharī paṇḍitāḥ

te te bhonti bahuśrutāḥ śrutidharāḥ saṃbuddhadharmaṃdharāḥkośān dharmamayān dharanti munināṃ
dharmānnidhāne ratāḥ |te te bhonti viśālaprajña vipulāṃ prīitiṃ janenti sadādeśentā varadharma
śānta nipuṇaṃ nairyāṇikaṃ durdṛśam

te te dharmamadharmajñeya matimān dharme sthitāḥ sūratāḥdharmarājyi praśāsi apratisamā varadharmacārī
sadā |te te bhonti viśiṣṭadharmagurukā gurugaurave ca sthitāḥdharme nagavare sthitā
matidharā dharmadhvajocchrāyikāḥ

te te matta pramatta sattva satataṃ dṛṣṭvā pramāde sthitāndṛṣṭvā caiva pranaṣṭa utpathagatān
saṃsāramārge sthitān |teṣū maitra janitvudāra karuṇā muditāpyupekṣā sthitāteṣāṃ mārgavaraṃ
pradarśayi śivamaṣṭāṅgikaṃ durdṛśam

te tu nāva karitva dharma sudṛḍhāṃ dhārenti sattvān bahūnudyantān mahārṇaveṣu patitān saṃsārasrotogatān
|bodhyaṅgā bala indriyaiḥ kavacitāḥ saddharmanāvāruhāḥtīre pārami kṣema nityamabhaye
sthāpenti sattvān sadā

te te vaidyavarā vrateṣu caritā vaidyottamā vedakāvidyājñānavimuktipāragamitā saddharmabhaiṣajyadāḥ
|dṛṣṭvā sattva gilāna nekavividhai rogaiḥ samabhyāhatānteṣāṃ dharmavirecanaṃ dadati
taddharmaiścikitsanti tān

te te vādi apavādimathanā lokendra vāgīśvarāḥsarvajñeyaprabhaṃkarā matidharā varajñānabhūmisthitāḥ
|śūra jñānabalā balapramathanāḥ saṃvarṇitā jñānibhiḥjñāneno bahusattvakoṭiniyutāṃstoṣyanti
dharme sthitāḥ

te te'dhipati sārthavāha vipadaḥ sattvāna trāṇārthikāḥdṛṣṭvā sattva pramūḍha mārgaratane sada
mārapāśe sthitāḥ |teṣāṃ mārgavaraṃ prakāśayi śivaṃ kṣemaṃ sadā nirvṛtīyena jñānapathena
nenti kuśalān bahusattvakoṭīśatān

te te lenu bhavanti trāṇu śaraṇaṃ cakṣuḥ pradīpaṃkarāḥbhītānāmabhayapradāśca satataṃ trastāna
cāśvāsakāḥ |te'tiduḥkhita sattva jñātva paramān jātyandhabhūtānimāndharmāloku karonti dharmaratane
bhūtanaye śikṣitāḥ

ye ye śilpavarā jage bahukarāḥ sattvāna arthāvahāyebhiḥ sattva sadā bhavanti sukhitāḥ śilpeṣu
saṃśikṣitāḥ |śikṣāpāramitāṃ gatāḥ sukuśalā āścaryaprāptādbhutāye bodhīnabhiprasthitā matidharā
lokasya cakṣurdadāḥ

no te tṛpta kadācidapratisamā varabuddhadharmaśrutāḥśīlakṣāntisamādhipāragamitā gambhīradharmaśrutāḥ
|no tṛptāśca pareṣu dharmaratanaṃ te deśayantaḥ śivaṃmokṣopāyu pravarṣamāṇu varṣaṃ
dharmairnarāṃstarpayī

yāvanto bahu sattva teṣupagatā dharmārthikāḥ paṇḍitāḥśroṣyāmo varadharmaśreṣṭharatanaṃ mārgaṃ
ṛjuṃ añjasam |teṣāṃ chindiṣu saṃśayān matiadharā dharmeṇa saṃtoṣayīśīlakṣāntisamādhipāramigatā
jānanta sattvaśayān

jñānī jñānavarāgra pāramigatāḥ sattvāśaye kovidāḥjānantaḥ parasattvacittacaritaṃ yeṣāṃ kathā
yādṛśī |ye ye jñānakathāya sattvanayutā varadharmacakṣurlabhāḥte te jñānaviśeṣapāramigatā
mārgopadeśaṃkarāḥ

mārā koṭisahasra teṣa viduṣāṃ cittaṃ pi no jāniṣuākāśe yatha pakṣiṇāṃ padagatiṃ jñātuṃ na śakyā
kvacit |śāntā dānta praśānta jñānavaśino āryasmi jñāne sthitāḥsarvān māra nihatya śūra
vṛṣabhā budhyanti bodhiṃ śivām

ṛddhipāramiprāpta bhonti satataṃ gacchanti kṣetrān śatānpaśyanti bahubuddhakoṭiniyutān gaṅgā
yathā vālikāḥ |cakṣusteṣa na sajjate daśadiśe paśyanti rūpān bahuye co sattva daśaddiśe bhavasthitāḥ
sarveṣa te nāyakāḥ

te tasyo bhaṇi ānuśaṃsa sakalāṃ kalpāna koṭīśatānno co pūrvacarīya varṇa kṣapaye pratibhānato
bhāṣato |buddhānāṃ dhanamakṣayaṃ suvipulaṃ jñānasya co sāgaraṃyo etaṃ virajaṃ samādhimatulaṃ
dhāreya kaścinnaraḥ

iti śrīsamādhirāje śīlaskandhanirdeśaparivartaḥ ṣaṭatriṃśatitamaḥ

samādhinirdeśaparivartaḥ |tatra khalu bhagavān punareva candraprabhaṃ kumārabhūtamāmantrayate
sma-tasmāttarhi kumāra bodhisattvena mahāsattvena anuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhimabhisaṃboddhukāmena
samādhinirdeśakuśalena bhavitavyam | tatra kumāra katamaḥ samādhinirdeśaḥ ? yā yathāvattatā
sarvadharmāṇāṃ samatā aviṣamatā | akalpanā avikalpanā | aviṭhapanā asamutthāpanā
| anutpādaḥ anirodhaḥ | kalpavikalpaparikalpasamucchedaḥ | cittānālambanatā | amanasikāraḥ
| prajñaptisamucchedaḥ | vitarkavikalpasamucchedaḥ | rāgadveṣamohasamucchedaḥ | nāntānantamanasikāraḥ
| manasikārasamucchedaḥ | skandhadhātvāyatanasvabhāvajñānam | smṛtimatigatihrīdhṛticāritrācāragocarapratipattisthānam
| araṇābhūmiḥ | śāntabhūmiḥ | sarvaprapañcasamucchedaḥ
| sarvabodhisattvaśikṣā | sarvatathāgatagocaraḥ | sarvaguṇapariniṣpattiḥ | ayamucyate
kumāra samādhinirdeśaḥ | yatra samādhinirdeśe pratiṣṭhito bodhisattvo mahāsattvo'virahito
bhavati samādhinā, abhrāntacittaśca bhavati, mahākaruṇāsamanvāgato'prameyāṇāṃ ca sattvānamarthaṃ
karoti ||atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ velāyāmimā gāthā abhāṣata-samādhyaviṣamā bhūmiḥ
śāntā sūkṣmā sudurdṛśā |sarvasaṃjñāsamuddhātaḥ samādhistena cocyate

akalpaścāvikalpaścāgrāhyatvamanidarśanam |anupalabdhiścittasya samādhistena cocyate

samāhito yadā bhoti sarvadharmā na manyate |amanyanā yathābhūtaṃ samādhiriti śabditaḥ

na dharme'sti rajomātra rajaścāpi na vidyate |anupalabdhirdharmāṇāṃ samādhistena cocyate

cittasyānupalabdhiśca vikalpo hyeṣa cocyate |avikalpitāśca te dharmā samādhireṣa jānathaḥ

śabdena sūcito hyarthaḥ sa ca śabdo avastukaḥ |pratiśrutkopamaḥ śabdo antarīkṣaṃ yathā nabhaḥ

asthitā hi ime dharmāḥ sthitireṣāṃ na vidyate |asthitiḥ sthitiśabdena svabhāvena na labhyate

cyavate agatītyevaṃ gatiścāsau na vidyate |agatirgatiśabdena samādhirnaditastathā

asamāhito vucyati eṣa manyanāsamāhito eṣa dvitīya manyanā |amanyamānā vicaranti bodhayeamanyamānā
spṛśi bodhimuttamām

samaviṣama eṣa śāntabhūmiḥśamathavipaśyanānimitta eṣā |seviya imu śānta buddhabodhiṃsa iha prayukta
samādhibhāvanāyām

na ca punariyamakṣarehi śakyaṃpraviśitu arthagati praveśe |sarvaruta jahitva bhāṣyaghoṣaṃbhavati
samāhita no ca manyanāsya

yaśca iha samādhi bodhisattvoyatha-upadiṣṭu tathā sthiheta yuktaḥ |sacediha bhavi kalpadāhu
kṣetregirivaramadhyagataṃ na taṃ dahe'gniḥ

yatha gaganu na jātu dagdhapūrvaṃsubahukalpaśatehi dahyamānam |gaganasamā adhijānamāna dharmāṃ-ste
na jātu dahyati so'gnimadhye

saci puna jvalamāna buddhakṣetrepraṇidhi karoti samādhiye sthihitvā |jvalanu ayu praśāmyatāmaśeṣaṃpṛthivī
vinaśyi na cāsya anyathātvam

ṛddhibalu anantu tasya bhotikhagapathi gacchati so asajjamānaḥ |imi guṇa anubhoti bodhisattvoyatha-anuśiṣṭu
samādhiye sthihitvā

jāyate cyavate vāpi na ca jāti na cyutiḥ |yasya vijānanā eṣā samādhyasya na durlabhā

na cyutirnāpi co jāti lokanāthena deśitā |lokanāthaṃ viditvaivaṃ samādhiṃ tena jānatha

anopaliptā lokena lokadharme na sajjati |asajjamānaḥ kāyena buddhakṣetrāṇi gacchati

kṣetreṣu paśyate nityaṃ saṃbuddhān lokanāyakān |dharmaṃ ca śṛṇute tatra buddhakṣetreṣu bhāṣitam

na jātu tasya ajñānaṃ dharmadhātuṃ ca bhāṣate |gatijñaḥ satato dharme dharmadhātumayo hi saḥ

bhāṣataḥ kalpakoṭyo'pi pratibhānaṃ na hīyate |nirmiṇoti bahūnanyān bodhisattvān vicakṣaṇaḥ

kṣetrātaḥ kṣetra gacchanti bodhisattvāna nirmitāḥ |sahasrapatrapadmeṣu paryaṅkena niṣaṇṇakāḥ

buddhabodhiṃ prakāśenti dhāraṇīsūtraśobhanam |anyāśca sūtrakoṭīyo samādhiṃ śānta bhāvayan

avivartikapathe sthāpenti bahūn sattvānacintiyān |pratibhānaṃ kṣayaṃ naiti buddhabodhiṃ prakāśiya

kūṭāgāre hi gacchanti ratanehi vicitrite |okiranti ca puṣpehi gandhavadbhirvināyakam

okiranti ca cūrṇehi gandhavantehi nāyakam |kurvanti vipulāṃ pūjāṃ sarve te bodhikāraṇāt

aprameyā guṇā ete bodhisattvāna tāyinām |niṣkileśā yadā bhonti tadā ṛddhiṃ labhanti te

anupattikileśāna acchāḥ śuddhāḥ prabhāsvarāḥ |asaṃskṛtā akopyāśca bodhisattvāna gocarāḥ

praśāntā upaśāntāśca niṣkileśā anaṅganāḥ |aprapañcā niṣprapañcāḥ prapañcasamatikramāḥ

apracāro'kṣarāṇāṃ ca sarvadharmāṇa lakṣaṇam |durvijñeyaśca ghoṣeṇa samādhistena cocyate

akṣayā upaśāntā ca anābhogā adarśanā |gocaraḥ sarvabuddhānāṃ bhūtakoṭiranāvilā

sarvabuddhāniyaṃ śikṣā sarvadharmasvabhāvatā |iha śikṣitva saṃbuddhā guṇānāṃ pāramiṃ gatāḥ

na saṃpāraṃ na vāpāraṃ pūrvānto na vikalpitaḥ |tena te sarva saṃbuddhā guṇānāṃ pāramiṃ gatāḥ

anāgatānagatikān dharmān jñātvā svabhāvataḥ |niṣprapañcānanābhogāṃstatra te pāramiṃ gatāḥ

iti śrīsamādhirāje samādhinirdeśaparivartastrayodaśaḥ

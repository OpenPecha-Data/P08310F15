anumodanāparivartaḥ ||tatra bhagavān punareva candraprabhaṃ kumārabhūtamāmantrayate sma-tasmāttarhiṃ
kumāra bodhisattvena mahāsattvenopāyakuśalena bhavitavyam | kathaṃ ca kumāra bodhisattvo
mahāsattva upāyakuśalo bhavati ? iha kumāra bodhisattvena mahāsattvena sarvasattvānāmantike
jñātisaṃjñā utpādayitavyā | sarvasattvānāmantike jñāticittamupasthāpya yaḥ sarvasattvānāṃ
kuśalamūlapuṇyaskandhastat sarvamanumodayitavyam | trirātryāstridivasasya sarvasattvānāṃ
kuśalamūlapuṇyaskandhamanumodya sarvajñatārambaṇena cittotpādena teṣāmeva sarvasattvānāṃ
niryātayitavyam | anena kuśalamūlena bodhisattvo mahāsattvaḥ kṣipramimaṃ samādhiṃ
pratilabhate, kṣipraṃ cānuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhimabhisaṃbudhyate ||atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ
velāyāmimā gāthā abhāṣata -sarve mama jñātaya eti sattvāḥyasteṣamastī pṛthu puṇyaskandhaḥ
|rātrestrirevaṃ divasasya ca trī-ranumodamī emu janitva cittam

anumodamī ye suviśuddhaśīlāye jīvitārthe na karonti pāpam |adhimuktisaṃpanna ya bodhisattvāanumodamī
teṣa ya kiṃci puṇyam

anumodamī yeṣa prasādu buddhedharme prasādo'sti tathaiva saṃghe |anumodamī ye sugatasya pūjāṃkurvanti
bodhiṃ pratikāṅkṣamāṇāḥ

anumodamī yeṣa na ātmadṛṣṭi-rna bhāvadṛṣṭirna ca jīvadṛṣṭiḥ |anumodamī yeṣa na pāpadṛṣṭi-rye
śūnyatāṃ dṛṣṭva janenti tuṣṭim

anumodamī ye sugatasya śāsanelabhanti pravrajyopasaṃpadaṃ ca |alpeccha saṃtuṣṭa vane vasantipraśāntacāritra
ye dhyānagocarāḥ

anumodamī ekaka ye'dvitīyāvane vasantī sada khaḍgabhūtāḥ |ājīvaśuddhāḥ sada alpakṛtyāye jñātrahetorna
na karenti kūhanām

anumodamī yeṣa na saṃstavo'stina cāpi īrṣyā na kuleṣu tṛṣṇā |uttrasti traidhātuki nityakālamanopaliptā
vicaranti loke

anumodamī yeṣa prapañcu nāstinirviṇṇa sarvāsu bhavopapattiṣu |avigṛhītā upaśāntacittāna durlabhasteṣa
samādhireṣaḥ

anumodamī ye gaṇadoṣa dṛṣṭvāsarvān vivādān parivarjayitvā |sevantyaraṇyaṃ vanamūlamāśritāvimuktisārāḥ
sugatasya putrāḥ

anumodamī ye viharantyaraṇyenātmānamutkarṣi parānna paṃsaye |anumodamī yeṣa pramādu nāstiye
apramattā ima buddhaśāsane

yāvanta dharmāḥ pṛthu bodhipākṣikāḥsarveṣa mūlaṃ hyayamapramādaḥ |ye buddhaputrāḥ sada apramattāna
durlabhasteṣa ayaṃ samādhiḥ

nidhānalābhaḥ sugatāna śāsanaṃpravrajyalābho dvitīyaṃ nidhānam |śraddhāya lābhastṛtīyaṃ nidhāna-mayaṃ
samādhiścaturthaṃ nidhānam

śratvā imaṃ śūnyata buddhagocaraṃtasyāpratikṣepu nidhānalambhaḥ |anantu pratibhānu nidhānalambhoyā
dhāraṇī tat paramaṃ nidhānam

yāvanti dharmāḥ kuśalāḥ prakīrtitāḥśīlaṃ śrutaṃ tyāgu tathaiva kṣāntiḥ |sarveṣa mūlaṃ hyayamapramādonidhānalambhaḥ
sugatena deśitaḥ

ye apramattā iha buddhaśāsanesamyak ca yeṣāṃ praṇidhānamasti |na durlabhasteṣa ayaṃ samādhi-rāsannabhūtā
iha buddhaśāsane

iti śrīsamādhirāje anumodanāparivarto nāma pañcaviṃśatitamaḥ ||

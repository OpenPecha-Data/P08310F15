pūrvāntajñānamaparāntajñānaṃtriyadhva-samatā sugatāna śāsane |pariccheda uktaḥ sa trimaṇḍalasyaevaṃ
jino deśayi dharmasvāmī

cittavyavasthāna ekāgratā cakāyavyavasthāna yathāryabhūmiḥ |īryāpathastho sada kāli rakṣaṇādeśeti
dharmaṃ puruṣarṣabho muniḥ

hiriśca otrāpyu prāsādikaṃ cayuktāṃ giraṃ bhāṣati lokajñānam |pravṛttidharmaṃ prakṛtiṃ ca prāṇināṃdeśeti
dharmaṃ varabodhiumagryām

anugrahaṃ co hirimotrāpyaṃ cacittasya cākuśalatā jugupsanā |dhūtasyānutsargata piṇḍacaryāṃdeśeti
dharmaṃ dvipadānamuttamaḥ

hiriśca otrāpyu sadācaretāgurūṇābhivādana pratyutthānam |mānasya co nigraha āditaivaevaṃ jino
deśayi dharmasvāmī

cittasamutthānata cittakalyatājñānapratīvedhu tathānubodham |ajñānapakṣasya sadā vivarjanādeśeti
dharmān varabuddhabodhim

cittapraveśaṃ ca rūtasya jñānaṃniruktyavasthāna viniścitārtham |sarveṣvanarthāna sadā vivarjanamevaṃ
jino deśayi dharmasvāmī

sasaṅgatā satpuruṣehi nityaṃvivarjanā kāpuruṣāna caiva |jine prasādaṃ sada prematāṃ caevaṃ jino
deśayi dharma śreṣṭham

saṃketaprajñaptivyāhāratāṃ casaṃsāraduḥkhāni sadā vivarjanā |alābhi lābhe ca asaktabhāva-mevaṃ
jino deśayi dharmamuttamam

satkāru labdhvā ca na vismayeyyāasatkṛtaścāpi bhavedupekṣakaḥ |bhūte'pi varṇaṃ na kadāci modiyeiya
deśanā lokahitasya īdṛśī

ākrośanāṃ paṃsana sarvaśo sahe-dasaṃstavaḥ sarvagṛhīhi sārdham |saṃsargatāṃ pravrajitena kuryā-devaṃ
jino deśayi dharmasvāmī

buddhāna co gocari supratiṣṭhitoagocaraṃ sarva vivarjayitvā |ācārasaṃpanna sudāntacittoiya dharmanetrī
sugatena deśitā

ye bāladharmāḥ sada tān vivarjayetkuladūṣaṇaṃ sarva vivarjayecca |ārakṣitavyaṃ sada buddhaśāsanaṃevaṃ
jino deśayi dharmasvāmī

alpaṃ ca bhāṣye madhuraṃ suyuktaṃkalyāṇatāṃ mṛduvacanaṃ pareṣām |pratyarthikānāṃ sahadharmanigrahoiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

pratikramet kāli na co akālena viśvaset sarvapṛthagjaneṣu |duḥkhena spṛṣṭo na bhaveta durmanāiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

daridra dṛṣṭvā sadhanaṃ kareyyāduḥśīla dṛṣṭvā anukampitavyā |hitavastutāyāṃ sada ovadeyyaiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

dharmeṇa sattvā anugṛhṇitavyālokāmiṣatyāgu sadā ca kāryo |na saṃcayaṃ no nicayaṃ ca kuryā-diyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

śīlapraśaṃsā ca kuśīlakutsanāaśāṭhyatā śīlavatāṃ niṣevaṇam |sarvasvakātyāgi dhane'pyaniśritoiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

adhyāśayeno guruṇā nimantraṇāyathā ca bhāṣe tatha sarva kuryām |abhīkṣṇa seveyya ca dharmabhāṇakaṃiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

sagauravaḥ prītamanāḥ sadā bhavetsomyāya dṛṣṭīya sadā sthito bhavet |pūrvāsu caryāsu suniścitaḥ
sadāiyaṃ jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

pūrvaṃgamaḥ kuśalacarīṣu nitya-mupāyakauśalya nimittavajane |saṃjñāvivarte tatha vastulakṣaṇeiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

sūtrāntanirhārapadeṣu kauśalaṃsatyāna nirdeśapadeṣu niścayaḥ |vimuktijñānasya ca sākṣikāritāiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

śūnyāśca dharmāḥ sada sevitavyāviśāradāḥ śīlabale pratiṣṭhitāḥ |samādhisthānena samottareyyāiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

na jñātralābhaṃ pi kadāci deśaye-ccittasya cāpī kuhanāṃ na kuryāt |dṛṣṭīkṛtāṃ sarva vivarjayeccaiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

pratibhānu śreṣṭhaṃ vara dhāraṇīyejñānasya cobhāsu anantapāro |adhiṣṭhānamantra pratibhānayukti-riyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

śīlasya dvāramima mārgabhāvanāpratipatti-ovāda-nayaśca bhadrako |anuśāsanī atra caritva śāsanīiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

anulomikī kṣānti ya buddhavarṇitākṣāntisthito doṣa vivarjayeta |ajñāna varjeyya sthihitva jñāneiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

jñānapratiṣṭhā tatha yogabhūmīyogeśvarī bodhayi prasthitānām |niṣevaṇā satpuruṣāṇa nityaṃiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

ayuktayogīna sadā vivarjanātathāgatairbhāṣita buddhabhūmi |anumoditā sarvihi paṇḍitehiiyaṃ jine
īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

bālaiḥ pratikṣipta ajñātakehiabhūbhiratra pṛthuśrāvakāṇām |parigṛhītāḥ sada bodhisattvaiḥiyaṃ
jine īdṛśa ānuśāsanī

tathāgatehi anubuddhametaṃdevehi co satkṛtu pūjitaṃ ca |anumoditaṃ brahmasahasrakoṭibhiḥkaccijjino
bhāṣati taṃ samādhim

nāgasahasrehi sadā susatkṛtaṃsuparṇayakṣehi ca kinnarehi |yā bhāṣitā bodhivarā jinebhiḥkaccijjino
bhāṣati taṃ samādhim

paryāpta yā nityu supaṇḍitehidhanaṃ ca śriṣṭhaṃ pravaraṃ sulabdham |nirāmiṣaṃ jñāna cikitsa
uttamākaccijjino bhāṣati taṃ samādhim

jñānasya koṣaḥ pratibhānamakṣayaṃsūtrāntakoṭīna praveśa eṣaḥ |parijña traidhātuki bhūtajñānaṃkaccijjino
bhāṣati taṃ samādhim

naukā iyaṃ deśita pāragāmināṃnāvā pi co oghagatāna eṣā |kīrtiryaśo vardhati varṇamālāyeṣāmayaṃ
śānta samādhi deśitaḥ

praśaṃsa eṣā ca tathāgatānāṃstavaśca eṣo puruṣarṣabhāṇām |stava bodhisattvāna nayaśca akṣayoyehī
ayaṃ śānta samādhi deśitaḥ

maitrī iyaṃ doṣaśame prakāśitāupekṣiyaṃ kāruṇikāna bhūmim |āśvāsayanteṣa mahāśayānāṃyeṣāṃ kṛtenaiṣa
samādhi bhāṣitaḥ

pratipattiyaṃ deśita siṃhanādinā-mitu buddhajñānasya varasya āgamaḥ |sarveṣa dharmāṇa svabhāvamudrāḥsamādhyayaṃ
deśitu nāyakehi

sarvajñajñānasya ca āharitrimācaryā iyaṃ bodhayi prasthitānām |vitrāsanaṃ māracamūya cāpisamādhyayaṃ
śānta jinena deśitaḥ

vidyā iyaṃ dharmasthitāna tāyināṃamitramadhye paramā ca rakṣā |pratyarthiṃkānāṃ sahadharmanigrahāḥsamādhyayaṃ
śānta jinena deśitaḥ

pratibhānabhūmī iya saṃprakāśitābalā vimokṣā tatha indriyāṇi |viśiṣṭa aṣṭādaśa buddhadharmāḥsamādhi
śānteṣa niṣevamāṇāḥ

daśāna paryeṣṭi balāna bhūtāpūrvanimittaṃ pi ca buddhajñāne |ye buddhadharmāḥ puruṣottamenaprakāśitā
lokahitānukampinā

buddhāna putrebhirayaṃ pratīhitovimokṣakāmānayu mārgu deśitaḥ |prītiśca tasmin sugatātmajānāṃśruṇitvimaṃ
śānta samādhi durdṛśam

yā buddhajñānasya ca pāripūritāyābheṣate paṇḍitu bodhisattvaḥ |viśuddhacittaśca śucirniraṅgaṇoimaṃ
niṣeveta samādhi śāntam

pariśuddha kāyo'sya yathā jinānavimokṣajñānaṃ ca vimuktidarśanam |asaṃkiliṣṭaḥ sada rāgabandhanaiḥimaṃ
niṣeveta samādhi bhadrakam

abhūmi doṣe vigamaśca mohejñānasya co āgamu muktimicchataḥ |vidyāya utpādu avidyanāśakamimaṃ
niṣeveta samādhi śāntam

vimuktisārāṇiya tṛpti bhāṣitādhyāyīnayaṃ śānta samādhi deśitaḥ |cakṣuśca buddhānamaninditānā-mimaṃ
niṣeveta samādhi śāntam

abhijña eṣā bahukṣetre darśitāṛddhiśca buddhāna ananta darśitā |yā dhāraṇī sāpi tato na durlabhāniṣevamāṇasya
samādhimetam

śāntendriyasyo iha sthānu bodhayeidamadhiṣṭhānamanantadarśitam |sūkṣmaṃ ca jñānaṃ vipulaṃ viśuddhaṃniṣevamāṇasya
imaṃ samādhim

su budhyate naiṣa ayuktayogai-rvivartanaṃ sarvasu akṣarāṇām |na śakyu ghoṣeṇa vijānanāyayeno
ayaṃ śānta samādhi na śrutaḥ

jñātaṃ tu vijñairayu bodhisattvai-ryathā va yaṃ deśitu dharmasvāminā |pratibuddhu śāntehi aninditehiimaṃ
samādhiṃ pratiṣevamāṇaiḥ

ārabdhavīryehi samudgṛhīta-mupasthitaṃ ca sāpi sadā sudhāritam |duḥkhakṣayo jātinirodhajñāna-mimaṃ
samādhiṃ pratiṣevamāṇaiḥ

sarveṣa dharmāṇamajāti bhāṣitāevaṃ ca sarvāsu bhavaggatīṣu |jñānāgru buddhāna mahāśayānāṃkaccijjino
bhāṣati taṃ samādhim

tasyo kumārasyimi gātha bhāṣatoaṣṭāśītiniyutasahasra pūrṇāḥ |ghoṣānugā kṣānti labhiṃsu tatraavivartikāye
sthitu buddhajñāne

dṛḍhabalastamavadī kumāra-madyāpi so tiṣṭhati lokanāthaḥ |pṛcchāmi tvaṃ dāraka etamarthaṃkutastvayā
eṣa śrutaḥ samādhiḥ

kumāru rājan avadī śṛṇohidṛṣṭasmi koṭīniyutaṃ jinānām |ekasmi kalpasmi te sarvi satkṛtāayaṃ
ca me śānta samādhi pṛcchitaḥ

catvāri kalpā navatiṃ ca anyekalpāna koṭīniyutā sahasrāḥ |jātismaro bhomyahu tatra tatrana cāpi
garbhe upapadyi jātu

tato mayā eṣā samādhi bhāvitaḥśuddhaṃ śrutasteṣa jināna bhāṣatām |śrutvā ca uddiṣṭu janetva
chandaṃniṣkāṅkṣaāptena spṛśiṣyi bodhim

ye bhikṣu mahyaṃ paripṛcchadentiparyāpuṇantasya imaṃ samādhim |upasthapemī ahu tatra gauravaṃyathaiva
lokārthakarāṇa antike

yeṣāṃ mayā antika eka gāthāuddiṣṭa caryāṃ caratānulomikīm |mānyāmi tānapyahu śāntu eteupasthapemī
ahu buddhagauravam

yaścāpi māṃ pṛcchitu kaścidetiparyāpuṇantaṃ imu satsamādhim |svapnāntare'pīha na me'sti kāṅkṣānāhaṃ
bhaviṣye jinu lokanāyakaḥ

vṛddheṣu madhyeṣu naveṣu bhikṣuṣusagauravo bhomyahu supratītaḥ |sagāravasyo mama vardhate yaśaḥpuṇyaṃ
ca kīrtiśca guṇāstathaiva

kalīvivādeṣu na bhomi utsukoalpotsuko bhomyahu tatra kāle |anyā gatirbhoti karitva pāpakaṃanyā
gatirbhoti karitva bhadrakam

ayuktayogāna asaṃyatānā-mamanojña teṣāṃ vacanaṃ śruṇitvā |karmasvako bhomyahu tasmi kālekṛtasya
karmasya na vipraṇāśaḥ

na hyatra krodho bhavatī parāyaṇaṃkṣāntībalaṃ gṛhṇayahu buddhavarṇitam |kṣāntiḥ sadā varṇita
nāyakehikṣāntiṃ niṣevitva na bodhi durlabhā

ahaṃ ca bhomī sada śīlavantoanyāṃśca śīlasmi pratiṣṭhapemi |śīlasya varṇu sadahaṃ bhaṇāmivarṇaṃ
ca bhoti mama tatra bhāṣitam

araṇyavarṇaṃ ca sadā pi bhāṣeśīlasmi co bhomi sadā pratiṣṭhitaḥ |samādapemi ahu anya poṣadhetāṃścaiva
bodhāya samādapemi

tān brahmacarye'pi samādapemiarthasmi dharmasmi pratiṣṭhapemi |teṣāṃ ca bodhimyahu bodhimārgaṃyasminnime
bhonti ananta saṅgāḥ

smarāmyahaṃ kalpamatītamadhvaniyadā jino āsi svarāṅgaghoṣaḥ |pratijña tasyo purataḥ kṛtā mekṣāntībalo
bhomyahu nityakālam

tatra pratijñāya pratiṣṭhihitvāvarṣāṇa koṭī caturo aśītim |māreṇahaṃ kutsita paṃsitaścana caiva
cittaṃ mama jātu kṣubdham

jijñāsanāṃ tatra karitva mārojñātvāna mahyaṃ dṛḍha kṣāntimaitrīm |prasannacittaścaraṇāni vandya
mepañcaśatā bodhivarāya prasthitāḥ

amatsarī bhomyahu nityakālaṃtyāgasya co varṇa sadā prabhāṣe |āḍhyaśca bhomī dhanavān mahātmādurbhikṣakāle
bahu bhomi dāyakaḥ

ye bhikṣu dhārenti imaṃ samādhiṃye cāpi vācenti ya uddiśanti |karomi teṣāṃ ahu pāricaryāṃsarve
ca bheṣyanti narāṇamuttamāḥ

karmaṇā tenāhamanuttareṇapaśyāmi buddhān bahu lokanāthān |labhitva pravrajya jinānuśāsanebhavāmi
nityaṃ vidu dharmabhāṇakaḥ

dhūtābhiyukto ahu bhomi nityaṃkāntāramāraṇya sadā niṣevī |nāhārahetoḥ kuhanāṃ karomisaṃtuṣṭu
bhomī itaretareṇa

anīrṣuko bhomyahu nityakālaṃkuleṣu cāhaṃ na bhavāmi niśritaḥ |kuleṣu saktasya hi īrṣya vardhateanīrṣyukastuṣṭi
vaneṣu vindami

maitrīvihārī ahu bhomi nitya-mākruṣṭu santā na janemi krodham |maitrīvihāriṣyami sūratasyacaturdiśaṃ
vardhati varṇamālā

alpecchu saṃtuṣṭu bhavāmi nitya-māraṇyakaścaiva dhutābhiyuktaḥ |na cotsṛjāmī ahu piṇḍapātaṃdṛḍhaṃ
samādhāna dhuteṣu vindami

śrāddhaśca bhomī manasaḥ prasādobahuprasādaḥ sada buddhaśāsane |prasāda bahu lapsyami ānuśaṃsāprāsādiko
bhomi ahīna indriyaḥ

yaścaiva bhāṣāmyahu tatra tiṣṭhepratipattisāro ahu nityu bhomi |pratipattisārasyimi devanāgāḥkurvantyusthānu
prasannacittāḥ

guṇā ime kīrtita yāvatā meete ca anye ca bahū aneke |ye śikṣitavyāḥ sada paṇḍitenayo icchatī
budhyitu buddhabodhim

smarāmyato bahutaru duṣkarāṇiye pūrvakalpe caritānyaneke |bahuṃ pi dānīṃ bhaṇitu na śakyaṃgacchāmi
tāvat sugatasya antikam

sutīkṣṇaprajño vidu bodhisattvotasmin kṣaṇe sparśayi pañcabhijñā |ṛddhīya so gacchi jinasya
antikesa prāṇikoṭībhiraśītibhiḥ saha

dṛḍhabalo paramu udagra āsītsārdhaṃ tadā koṭiśatehi ṣaṣṭibhiḥ |upasaṃkramī mūlu tathāgatasyavanditva
pādau purato nyaṣīdat

adhyāśayaṃ tasya viditva rājñoimaṃ samādhiṃ dvipadendra deśayi |śrutvā ca so pārthivimaṃ samādhiṃujjhitva
rājyaṃ nirapekṣu pravrajī

sa pravrajitvāna imaṃ samādhiṃbhāveti vāceti prakāśayeti |sa ṣaṣṭibhiḥ kalpasahasru paścātpadmottaro
nāma jino abhūṣi

ṣaṣṭistadā koṭiśata anūnakāye rājña sārdhaṃ upasaṃkramī jinān |te cāpi śrutvaiva samādhimetaṃtuṣṭā
udagrāstada pravrajiṃsu

te pravrajitvāna imaṃ samādhiṃdhāreṃsu vāceṃsu prakāśayiṃsu |ṣaṣṭīna kalpāni nayutāna atyayāspṛśiṃṣu
bodhiṃ varamekakalpe

anantajñānottaranāmadheyāabhūṣi buddhā naradevapūjitāḥ |tadekameke dvipadānamuttamomocenti sattvānyatha
gaṅgavālikāḥ

śīrībalo rāju ahaṃ abhūṣiimāṃ caranto varabodhicārikām |ye mama putrāḥ śata pañca āsanimameva
ete anudharmapāpāḥ

evaṃ mayā kalpasahasrakoṭayoārabdhavīryeṇa atandritena |samādhi paryeṣṭa ayaṃ viśuddhaḥsamudānayanneti
tamagrabodhim

kumāra tasmāddhi ye bodhisattvāākāṅkṣate etu samādhimeṣitum |ārabdhavīryo nirapekṣu jīvitemamā
kumārā anuśikṣate sadā

iti śrīsamādhirāje bahubuddhanirhārasamādhimukhaparivarto nāma saptadaśaḥ ||

smitasaṃdarśanaparivartaḥ |atha khalu candraprabhaḥ kumārabhūta utthāyāsanādekāṃsamuttarāsaṅgaṃ
kṛtvā dakṣiṇaṃ jānumaṇḍalaṃ pṛthivyāṃ pratiṣṭhāpya yena bhagavāṃstenāñjaliṃ praṇamya bhagavantametadavocat-āścaryaṃ
bhagavan yāvat subhāṣiteyaṃ bhagavatā tathāgatenārhatā samyaksaṃbuddhena
sarvadharmasvabhāvasamatā sarvabodhisattvaśikṣāsamādhinirdeśaḥ | yathāpi nāma
bhagavan dīrgharātramatra śikṣitvā samudāgato'nuttarāyāṃ samyaksaṃbodhau | pratibhāti ca
me bhagavan, pratibhāti ca me sugata | bhagavānāha- pratibhātu te kumāra yasyedānīṃkālaṃ manyase
| atha khalu candraprabhaḥ kumārabhūto bhagavatā kṛtāvakāśo bhagavantaṃ saṃmukhaṃ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhyaṣṭāvīt-dṛṣṭvāna
sattvān dukhitānupadrutānrāgeṇa doṣeṇa sadābhibhūtān
|cittaṃ tvayotpāditu bodhikāraṇādūbuddho bhaveyaṃ ti prajāna mocakāḥ

cīrṇo'si vīrye bahukalpakoṭiyodāne dame saṃyami nityu śikṣitaḥ |śīle ca kṣāntau tatha vīrye'tandritodānaṃ
ca dattaṃ vipulamanantakam

na co tava mānasu jātu khinnaṃhastāṃśca pādāṃstyajamānu jīvitam |hiraṇyasuvarṇaṃ tatha putradāraṃrājthaṃ
ca tyaktamanapekṣa bhūtvā

śīlaṃ tavācchaṃ vimalaṃ viśuddhamātmā ca tyakto na ca śīla khaṇḍitam |kāyena vācā manasā susaṃvṛtāsudāntacittā
sugatā namo'stu te

kṣāntīratāḥ kṣāntipathe pratiṣṭhitāḥkāye kṛte khaṇḍa pi naiva krudhyase |kṣīraṃ tataḥ prasravi
maitrabhāvanāāścaryabhūtā sugatā namo'stu te

balairupetā daśabhirbalaiḥ sthitāasaṅgujñānī vidi sarvadharmān |karuṇāni lokahitakara dharmasvāminanukampase
prajā ima arthakāmaḥ

śūnyaṃ ti jñānaṃ na ca punihāsti sattvolokaṃ ca dṛṣṭva tathā ti pranaṣṭamārgam |vibodhitāste
prakṛtinirātmadharmevimuktijātā na ca kvaci sā vimuktiḥ

pratyādiśaṃ jahiya sadā pramattaṃjitvā ca māraṃ sabalamanantasainyam |buddhitva bodhi vipulāmanantajñānaṃdiśehi
dharmaṃ parama viśuddhaśāntam

gaganaṃ pateyyā saha śaśitārakehipṛthivī vinaśyet sanagaraśailasaṃsthā |ākāśadhāturapi ca siyānyathātvaṃno
caiva tubhyaṃ vitatha bhaṇeyya vācā

dṛṣṭvā tvaṃ duḥkhitān sattvānupalambharatāḥ prajāḥ |anopalambhaṃ deśesi gambhīrāṃ śāntaśūnyatām

śikṣito'si mahāvīra kalpakoṭīracintiyā |anopalambhaśikṣāyāṃ skhalitaṃ te na vidyate

yādṛśe śikṣito dharme tādṛśaṃ dharmu bhāṣase |abhūmiratra bālānāṃ yāvanta anyatīrthikāḥ

ye sthitā ātmasaṃjñāyāṃ te skhalanti avidvasu |jñātvā dharmāṇa nairātmyaṃ skhalitaṃ te na vidyate

bhūtavādī mahāvīra bhūtadharmapratiṣṭhitaḥ |bhūte satye sthito nātha bhūtāṃ vācaṃ prabhāṣase

bhūtā te cārikā āsīd yathā te praṇidhiḥ kṛtaḥ |tasya bhūtasya niṣyandā bhūtāṃ vācaṃ prabhāṣase

bhūtacaryāsu saṃpanno bhūtakoṭīsuśikṣitā |bhūtāśayā bhūtacarī bhūtaprajña namo'stu te

samaste prajñayā nāsti jñānavādi prabhākara |jñāne viśeṣatāṃ prāpta jñānavādi namo'stu te

mitrastvaṃ sarvasattvānāṃ maitrī tava subhāvitā |aprakampyo yathā meruracalaḥ supratiṣṭhitaḥ

gaṇe suvipule śāsturgaṇān saṃparikarṣasi |gabhīraprajñā sugatā nadase pariṣadgatā

siṃhanādaṃ nadi buddhaḥ siṃhavikrāntavikramaḥ |jitāste tīrthikāḥ sarve siṃhena kroṣṭukā yathā

adāntadamako vīra adāntā damitāstvayā |te ca mitrā dṛḍhā bhonti abhedyā bhonti susthitāḥ

dṛṣṭvā tvaṃ duḥkhitān sattvānātmadṛṣṭisamāśritān |nairātmya dharmaṃ deśemi yatra nāsti priyāpriyam

aśikṣitānāṃ bālānāṃ kumārgapathacāriṇām |mārgaṃ tvaṃ saṃprakāśesi yena gacchanti nāyakāḥ

ye sthitā ātmasaṃjñāyāṃ duḥkhe te supratiṣṭhitāḥ |na te jānanti nairātmyaṃ yatra duḥkhaṃ na
vidyate

akhilitapadadharmadeśako'siskhalitu na labhyati lokanātha |avitatha gira saṃprabhāṣase tvaṃduḥkhamokṣakarā
namaste nātha

bahuniyutaśatā sahasrakoṭyogaganasthitāḥ pṛthu devanāgayakṣāḥ |sarviṃ spṛha janenti nāyakasminbhagavatu
vāca śruṇitva arthayuktām

snigdhamṛdumanojñakālayuktāṃsumadhura vāca praṇīta premaṇīyām |aparimitasvarāṅgasaṃprayuktāṃhitakara
mokṣakarīṃ bahujanasya

turiyaśatasahasra aprameyāsumadhura yukta bhaveyurekakāle |divyasvara viśiṣṭa premaṇīyāabhibhavati
sugatasya ekavācā

dvijagaṇakalaviṅkamañjudhoṣāḥsurucira premaṇīyāḥ sugītaśabdāḥ |śaṅkhapaṭahabherivīṇaśabdāḥkalamapi
na labhantiḥ buddhaśabde

parabhṛtaśukaśārikāṇa śabdā-statha punaḥ krauñcamayūrakinnarāṇām |ruta ravita ya keci premaṇīyāḥkalamapi
buddhasvarasya nānubhonti

priya madhura manojña premaṇīyāḥsumadhura śāntagirā praśaṃsanīyāḥ |sarvi gira prayukta ekakālegiravara
harṣaṇiyāstathāgatasya

suramanujanarendradānavānāṃsakalabhave tribhave ya asti sattvāḥ |yā prabhā abhavat prabhākarāṇāṃabhibhavati
sugatasya ekaraśmiḥ

kusumitu sugatasya ātmabhāvaḥparivṛtu vicitru sarvalakṣaṇaiḥ |puṇyaśatanirvṛtu accha śuddhaḥpratapati
sarvajage jinasya kāyaḥ

śaṅkhāna śabda paṇavasughoṣakāṇāṃbherīṇa śabda tathapi ca kimpalānām |sarve ca śabda sumadhura
premaṇīyābuddhasya śabde śatima kalāṃ na bhonti

tūryāṇa koṭiniyutasahasraśabdāāsvādanīya sumadhura divyakalpāḥ |prāmodanīya marugaṇa apsarāṇāṃbuddhasya
śabde śatima kalāṃ na bhonti

krauñcā mayūra parabhṛta cakravākāhaṃsāḥ kuṇālā bahuvidhapakṣisaṃghāḥ |ye te saśabdāḥ sumadhura
ekakālebuddhasya śabde śatima kalāṃ na bhonti

nāgāna yakṣāṇa asuramahoragāṇāṃdevendrabrahmamarupatīnāṃ ca śabdāḥ |yāvanta śabdāstribhave manojña
kāntābuddhasvarasya kalamapi te na bhonti

yā brahmaṇo vā marupatinaśca ābhāprabhāsvarāṇāṃ maṇiratanāna ābhāḥ |sarvā ya ābhā vividhamanekarūpāḥsarvāsta
ekā abhibhavi buddharaśmiḥ

kāyena śuddho vacasā manena caivajñānena śuddhastribhavi anopaliptaḥ |guṇasārarāśi guṇaratano
narendraḥsarvaguṇehi asamasamaḥ svayaṃbhūḥ

evaṃ stavitvā daśabala satyavādiṃvācaṃ prabhāṣi muditamanaḥ kumāraḥ |pūjitva buddhamatuliyamaprameyaṃbuddho
bhaveya yatha iva śākyasiṃhaḥ

tasyo viditvā sugatu viśiṣṭa caryā-masaṅgajñānī smitamakaronnarendraḥ |maitreyu pṛcchī daśabalajyeṣṭhaputraṃkasyārthi
etaṃ smitu kṛtu nāyakena

ākampitābhūdvasumati ṣaḍvikāraṃdevāśca nāga gaganasthitā udagrāḥ |prekṣanti buddhaṃ pramudita
hṛṣṭacittā-staṃ vyākarohi sugata anābhibhūtaḥ

abhūmirasmi bhagavatu śrāvakāṇāṃyatra pravṛttaṃ puruṣavarasya jñānam |suviśuddhajñāninnanupamaprajñabhūmiakhilā
te smitu kṛtu jina kasya arthe

pṛcchami daśabalaṃ vināyakaṃśākyasiṃha dvipadānamuttamam |jñānapāramiṃ gataṃ prabhākaraṃrāgadveṣakhilamohasādakam

kalpakoṭi carito'si nāyakogaṅgavālukasamāstatottaram |eṣamāṇu varabodhimuttamāṃkasya arthi smitu
etu darśitam

hasta-pāda parikṛtta śāstunāputradāra priyajñāti bāndhavān |eṣamāṇu varajñānamuttamaṃko nu hetu
smitadarśane mune

aśvahastirathapattiyo tvayādāsadāsimaṇiratna rūpyakam |naiva dravyaratanaṃ ca labhyateyanna
tyaktu caratā ti cārikām

jñānu śreṣṭhu tribhave'tivartatesarvasattvacariyāṃ prajānase |dhātucittu adhimuktikovidākasya
arthi smitu etu darśitam

kena pūjita narāṇamuttamāḥkasya vārtha vipulo bhaviṣyati |ko ca asya cariyāya grāhakaḥkasya
arthi smitu darśitaṃ mune

ṣaḍvikāra pṛthivī prakampitāpadmakoṭya dharaṇītu utthitāḥ |koṭipatraparamā prabhāsvarāhemavarṇarucirā
manoramā

yatrime sthita jinasya aurasābodhisattva paramā maharddhikāḥ |dharmabhāṇaka bahū samāgatā-steṣa
kāruṇika pṛcchi nāyakam

bheriśaṅkhatuṇavāḥ sughoṣakā-stūrya koṭiniyutāḥ pravāditāḥ |teṣa śabda gaganasmi śrūyateyādṛśaḥ
sugataghoṣa acintiyaḥ

haṃsakrauñcakalaviṅkakokilāḥpakṣisaṃgha bahukāḥ samāgatāḥ |muñci ghoṣa paramaṃ prabhāsvaraṃbuddhaghoṣakala
nānubhonti te

kena dāna dama saṃyamaḥ purekalpakoṭi bahukā niṣevitāḥ |kena pūjita narāṇa uttamākasya arthi
smitu etu darśitam

kena pūrvi dvipadendru pṛcchitogauravaṃ paramu saṃjanitvana |buddhabodhi kathameṣa labhyatekasya
arthi smitu etu darśitam

yāttikā daśabalā atītakāḥpratyutpanna sugatā anāgatāḥ |sarva jānasi narāṇamuttamotena pṛcchami
prajāya kāraṇāt

cittasaṃtati prajāya jānatesarvi prāṇina anantagocarāḥ |yasya yādṛśu narasya āśaya-stena pṛcchami
narāṇamuttamam

ye caranti cariyāmanuttamāṃhetuyuktivinayasmi kovidāḥ |buddhajñāna kathametu labhyateetadarthi
dvipadendru pṛcchiham

ye hi dharma sukhumāḥ sudurdṛśāḥśūnya śānta atulā acintiyāḥ |bhāvitā daśabalāna gocarā-steṣa
artha ahu pṛcchi nāyakam

yeṣa maitri karuṇā subhāvitāsarvaprāṇiṣu jage acintiyā |sattvasaṃjña na ca yeṣa vartateteṣa
arthi dvipadendru pṛcchiyām

yeṣa jñānamatulamacintiyaṃteṣa grāhya na kadāci vidyate |cittagocariya pāramiṃ gatāteṣa arthi
ahu nātha pṛcchami

śīlajñānaguṇapāramiṃ gatātryadhvajñānamatulaṃ bhivartate |naiva tubhya skhalitūpalabhyatekasya
arthi smitu etu darśitam

śāriputra aniruddha kolitāye ca anya sugatasya śrāvakāḥ |naiva teṣa iha jñānu vartatebuddhagocaru
ayaṃ nirūttaraḥ

sarvadharmavaśipāramiṃ gatāḥsarvaśikṣa-cariyāya udgatāḥsaṃjanetva karuṇāṃ vināyakāmuñca ghoṣa
paramārthakovidā

ye'pi pūrva bahukalpakoṭiyoeva cinti dvipadendru pṛcchitaḥ |bhaṣyamagru śaraṇaṃ parāyaṇaṃteṣa
adya phala brūhi nāyakā

yakṣarākṣasakumbhāṇḍaguhyakāḥprekṣamāṇa dvipadānamuttamam |sarvi prāñjalisthitāḥ sagauravāḥśrotu
vyākaraṇamagrapudgalān

bodhisattva bahavo'dya āgatāṛddhimanta bahukṣetrakoṭibhiḥ |jeṣṭhaputra sugatasya aurasāḥsarvi
prāñjalisthitāḥ sagauravāḥ

gandhahasti purimādiśā gato-'kṣobhyakṣetra diśi lokaviśrutaḥ |bodhisattvanayutaiḥ puraskṛtaḥśākyasiṃhu
dvipadendru pṛcchanā

sukhāvatīya varalokadhātutomahāsthāma prāpta avalokiteśvaraḥ |bodhisattvanayutaiḥ puraskṛtaḥśākyasiṃhu
dvipadendru pṛcchanā

yena pūrva bahukalpakoṭiyoaprameya sugatā upasthitāḥ |sāgarāṇa sakalā ca vālikāeṣatā parama
jñānamuttamam

sarvabuddhastuta saṃpraśaṃsitaḥsarvadharmaguṇapāramiṃ gataḥ |sarvalokadiśatāsu viśrutomañjughoṣa
sthitu prāñjalīkṛtaḥ

buddhakṣetraniyutaiścaritvanāsudurlabhamīdṛśakāna darśanam |buddhaputraguṇavat suśikṣitāḥsarvi
prāñjalisthitāḥ sagauravāḥ

nāsti anya iha kaści bhājanaṃevarūpi yatha eta sūratāḥ |dharmakoṣadhara sarvaśāstunāṃsnigdhabhāva
gira muñca nāyakā

na hyakāraṇaka jinā vināyakādarśayanti smitamagrapudgalāḥ |muñca ghoṣavara dundubhisvarakasya
arthi smitu etu darśitam

haṃsakokilamayūrasārasāmeghanāda vṛṣabhāḥ pragarjitāḥ |divyavādyamadhurāḥ pravāditāvyākarohi
gira sattvamocanī

maitrasaṃjanani premavardhanījñānadarśani avidyariñcanī |arthatīraṇi prajñāvivardhanīkalpakoṭiniyutā
viśodhanī

viniścitabhāvavibhāvita-duḥkhanirodhapadārthanidarśanī |sarvakutīrthakavādadhvaṃsanīśūnya nisattva
nijīva vibhāvani

puṇyasahasraśatehi alaṃkṛtaḥbuddhasahasraśatehi caritviha |devasahasraśatehi susaṃstutaḥbrahmasahasraśatehi
namaskṛtaḥ

rākṣasayakṣakumbhāṇḍaprasādanināgasuparṇamahoragamocani |nityamasakta-prayukta-udīraṇikarmaphalehi
śubhehi samudgataḥ

ye ca keci jināḥ parinirvṛtāye ca anāgata ye ca avasthitāḥ |sarvi prajānasi saṅgu na te'stītisarvaguṇehi
samudgata nāyaka

bhūtadharā sasamudraparvatasarvi mahī ṣaḍvikāra prakampitā |devagaṇā nabhi puṣpa kṣipanti cadivyu
pravāyati gandhu manoramu

hatarāgadoṣatimirā nikhilāpariśuddhaśīla pariśuddhamanāḥ |praśānta śūnya animittaratānarasiṃhanādu
nada kāruṇikā

pratibhānavanta suviśālayaśāsusamāptaprajña tathajñāna jinā |tava loki nāsti samu kāruṇikābhaṇa
kasyu arthi smitu darśayase

kalaviṅkakokilamayūraravā-statha jīvaṃjīvaṃ jīvakamanojñarutāḥ |rañjanīya śabdu bhuvi ekakṣaṇekala
tena bhonti sugatasya svare

bheryo mṛdaṅgapaṇavāśca tathāśaṅkha saveṇu tathā vallariyo |tūryāsahasra siya ekaravāḥkala nānubhonti
sugatasya rute

tūryasahasra vara divyarutārañjanīya gīta siya apsarasām |sugīti śabdarati saṃjanekala na bhonti
sugatasya rute

ekasvarā tu tava lokahitānānādhimukti svaru niścarati |ekaiku manyi mama bhāṣi jinobrūhi smitaṃ
ti kṛtu kasya kṛte

devāna śabda tatha nāgarutāye cāpi kinnararutā madhurāḥ |praśamenti kleśa na kadācidapibuddhasvarāstu
sada kleśanudāḥ

prītiṃ janeti na ca rāgaratiṃmaitrīṃ janeti na ca doṣamatim |prajñāṃ janeti na ca moharatiṃbuddhāna
sarva malanāśi svaraḥ

na bahi ca śabdu pariṣātu prajāsarveṣa chindati sa kāṅkṣaśatān |na ca onato na hi ca aunnamatosamasaukhyadarśana
svaro muninaḥ

bhajyādiyaṃ mahī saśailaraṇākṣīyate sāgarajalaṃ ca tathā |candro'tha sūryu dharaṇīṃ prapatedgiramanyathā
na puna bhāṣi jinaḥ

sarvāṅgavākya pariśuddhagirāsiṃhasvarā madhuramañjagirā |brahmasvarā sugata kāruṇikābhaṇa kasya
arthi smitu darśayase

yāvanta sattva iha sarva jagesarveṣa citta carate kuśalaḥ |ye atītanāgata ye sāṃpratikābhaṇa
kasya arthi smitu darśayase

yāvanta kecijjina kāruṇikājñānasmi sarvi vaśi pāramiṃ gatāḥ |na ca te jinā vimalacandramukhānāhaitukaṃ
smita sada darśayase

api kalpakoṭi bhaṇi apratimāyatha gaṅgavālika bhaṇeyya guṇān |na ca śakyu kīrtitu pramāṇu guṇebhaṇa
kasya arthi smitu darśayase

iti śrīsamādhirāje smitasaṃdarśanaparivartaścaturdaśaḥ

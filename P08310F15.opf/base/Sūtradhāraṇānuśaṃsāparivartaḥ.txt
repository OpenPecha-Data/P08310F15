na so'sti keṣucid vyūhaḥ sarvakṣetreṣu sarvaśaḥ |yo neha dṛśyate kṣetre tadviśiṣṭatamastadā

peyālametadākhyātaṃ śākyasiṃhena tāyinā |na te jñāne'tra kāṅkṣati bodhisatvā mahāyaśāḥ

koṭīya etāṃ budhyanti gatisteṣāmacintiyā |jñānena te vivardhante sāgaro vā sravantibhiḥ

na teṣāṃ labhyate'nto hi pibato vā mahodagheḥ |ākhyāto bodhisattvānāṃ nayo hyeṣa acintiyaḥ

iha koṭayāṃ sthitāḥ śūrā bodhisattvā yaśasvinaḥ |svarāṅgāni pramuñcanti yathā gaṅgāya vālikāḥ

tataścintyaḥ svaro'pyevaṃ bodhisattvo na manyate |manyanāyāṃ prahīṇāyāmāsanno bhoti bodhate

na sa śīlaṃ vilumpeti api jīvitakāraṇāt |aviluptaḥ sa carati bodhisattvo dṛḍhavataḥ

nāsau bhūyo vilupyeta kamasaṃjñāya sarvaśaḥ |sarvasaṃjñāprahīṇasta aprameyāḥ samādhayaḥ

samāhitaḥ sa carati sajjate na samādhiṣu |asaktaścāpramattaśca nāsau lokeṣu sajjate

lokadhātūnatikramya sa gacchati sukhāvatīm |gataśca tatra saṃbuddhamamitābhaṃ sa paśyati

bodhisattvāṃśca tān śūrān lakṣaṇaiḥ samalaṃkṛtān |pañcābhijñāpāramiṃ ca prāptā dhāraṇigocarāḥ

gacchanti kṣetrakoṭīyo buddhānāṃ pādavandakāḥ |obhāṣayanto gacchanti buddhakṣetrānacintiyān

sarvadoṣaprahīṇāśca sarvakleśaviśodhitāḥ |sarvakleśasamucchinnā ekajātisthitā jināḥ

na co apāyān gacchanti tasmāt kṣetrāttu te narāḥ |sarve'pāyā samucchinnāstasmin kṣetre aśeṣataḥ

bodhitā buddhaśreṣṭhena amitābhena tāyinā |karotha mā tatra kāṅkṣāṃ gamiṣyatha sukhāvatīm

yaḥ kṣetraśreṣṭhasya śruṇitva varṇaṃcittaprasādaṃ pratilabhi mātṛgrāmaḥ |sa kṣipra bhotī puruṣavaraḥ
suvidvānṛddhyā ca yāti kṣetrasahasrakoṭīḥ

yāvanti pūjā bahuvidha aprameyāyā kṣetrakoṭīnayutayabiṃbareṣu |tāṃ pūja kṛtva puruṣavareṣu nityaṃsaṃkhyākalāpī
na bhavati maitracittaḥ

śīlaṃ samādhiṃ satatu niṣevamāṇodhyānān vimokṣāṃstathapi ca apramāṇān |śūnyānimittān satatu
niṣevamāṇonacireṇa so hi sugatu bhavati loke

eṣā hi pūjā paramā viśiṣṭa mahyaṃyaḥ śīlaskandhe pratiṣṭhitu bodhisattvo |sada sarvabuddhāstena
supūjitā hikṣayāntakāle yaḥ sthitu bodhicitte

suparīnditāste buddhasahasrakoṭyoye bodhisattvā imu kṣayi kāli ghore |rakṣanti dharmaṃ sugatavaropadiṣṭaṃte
mahya putrāścarimaka dharmapālāḥ

iti śrīsamādhirāje sūtradhāraṇānuśaṃsāparivarto nāma dvātriṃśatitamaḥ

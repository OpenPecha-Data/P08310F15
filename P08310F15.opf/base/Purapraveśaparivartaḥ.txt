deva-asura-nāga-yakṣa-saṃghāgaruḍa-mahoraga-kinnarāḥ kumbhāṇḍāḥ |tathapi ca bahu pretapūtanāścopuruṣavarasya
karonti citrikāram

te'pi ca jinavare karitva pūjāṃnagaravaraṃ praviśanti nāyakasmin |deva asuranāgayakṣarājā |satatamatṛpta
bhavanti darśanena

yatha purimabhaveṣu lokanāthaḥpurimajineṣu akārṣi pūja śreṣṭhām |puṇyaphalavipāka evarūpona
ca janu tṛptu narendra paśyamānaḥ

meru tatha sumeru cakravālāhimagiristatha gandhamādanaśca |āvaraṇā na te jinasya bhontiābha
yadā jinu muñci buddhakṣetre

ye ca iha samudra buddhakṣetrete'pi mahīya samāstadā bhavanti |sarvamimu samantu buddhakṣetraṃsamu
bhavatī kusumehi saṃprakīrṇam

raśmi śatasahasra aprameyāavakiri pādatalehi dharmarājā |sarvi niraya śītalā bhavantidharmaduḥkha
upanīta sukhaṃ ca vedayanti

dharma daśabala saṃprabhāṣi tatromarumanujāna viśuddha bhoti cakṣuḥ |prāṇi śatasahasra aprameyāniyata
bhavanti ca sarvi buddhajñāne

bahu imi sugatasya pratihāryāna sukaru vaktu ca kalpakoṭiyebhiḥ |puravara praviśanti nāyakasminpramudita
sarva jagajjinapraveśe

imi guṇa sugatasya aprameyānaravṛṣabhasya guṇāgrapāragasya |sarvaguṇaviśeṣapāragasyaśirasi namasyatha
buddhapuṇyakṣetram

iti śrīsamādhirāje purapraveśaparivarto nāma daśamaḥ ||

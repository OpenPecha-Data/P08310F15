evaṃ prabhāṣita jinena girāahu tena bhomi paricīrṇa sadā |labhate ca dhāraṇi viśiṣṭa varā-mimu
yaḥ samādhi naru dhārayatī

kālakriyāṃ ca sa karoti yadāamitābhu tasya purataḥ sthihatī |bhikṣugaṇena saha kāruṇikoimu yaḥ
samādhi naru dhārayatī

lābhī ca dhāraṇiya so bhavatīdharmanidhāna vaśipāragataḥ |pratibhānavānanācchedyagiroya imaṃ
samādhi naru dhārayatī

yenaiva so vrajati dharmadharoālokabhūta bhavatī jagataḥ |sipraśāntacara suviśuddhamanāimu yaḥ
samādhi naru dhārayatī

vara dharmakośa vividhaṃ nipuṇaṃso dharmakāya vaśi pāragataḥ |so saṃśayaṃ chinatti sarvajageimu
yaḥ samādhi naru dhārayatī

sarve'pi sattva siya kāruṇikābhagavān bhavāntakaraṇe sugataḥ |tān satkareyyā bahu kalpaśatānyatha
gaṅgavāluka tathottari vā

yaścaiva paści kṣayakāli imaṃśrutvā samādhimiha kaści naraḥ |anumodamīti bhaṇataikagiraṃkala
puṇyaskandha na sa pūrva bhavet

yasyo kumāra iya śāntagatīparamārthaśūnyata samādhivaro |prāvartu bhoti tatha pustagataḥso dharmabhāṇaku
sthitaḥ sumatiḥ

iti śrīsamādhirāje tejaguṇarājaparivarto nāmonatriṃśatitamaḥ

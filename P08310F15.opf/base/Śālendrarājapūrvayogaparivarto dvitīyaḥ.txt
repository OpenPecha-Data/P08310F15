śālendrarājapūrvayogaparivarto dvitīyaḥ |atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ velāyāṃ candraprabhasya
kumārabhūtasya etadeva pūrvayogaparivartaṃ bhūyasyā mātrayā gāthābhigītena vistareṇa saṃprakāśayati
sma -smarati daśabalāna ṣaṣṭikoṭīpurimabhave nivasiṃsu gṛdhrakūṭe |puri mama caramāṇu
bodhicaryā-mima varaśānta samādhi deśayiṃsu

teṣāṃ paścimako āsīllokanāthaḥ prabhaṃkaraḥ |śālendrarāja nāmena sa mayā paripṛcchitaḥ

ahaṃ ca kṣatriyo āsaṃ rājaśreṣṭho mahīpatiḥ |maṃma co śata putrāṇāṃ pañcābhūvannanūnakāḥ

koṭīmayā vihāraṇāṃ tasya buddhasya kāritā |candanasya viśiṣṭasya kecidratnamayā abhūta

priyo manāpaśca bahujanasyabhīṣmottaro nāma abhūṣi rājā |akārṣi buddhasya viśiṣṭapūjāaṣṭādaśavarṣasahasrakoṭyaḥ

jinasya tasya dvipadottamasyaśālendrarājasya vināyakasya |ṣaṭūsaptativarṣasahasrakoṭiyoāyustadā
āsi aninditasya

niyutāntaśītisahasra śrāvakāṇāṃtraividya ṣaḍabhijña jitendriyāṇām |kṣīṇāsravāṇāntimadehadhāriṇāṃsaṃghastadā
āsi narottamasya

bahuprakārā mayi tasya pūjākṛtā jinasya dvipadottamasya |arthāya lokasya sadevakasyaimaṃ samādhiṃ
pratikāṅtā sadā

saputradāreṇa mi pravrajitvāśālendrarājasya jinasya antike |caturdaśavarṣasahasrakoṭiyoayaṃ
samādhiḥ paripṛcchito mayā

aśīti gāthā niyutā sahasrā-ṇyanye ca koṭīśata bimbarāṇām |tasyodgṛhītaḥ sugatasya antikā-ditaḥ
samādheḥ parivarta eṣaḥ

hastā śirā bhārya tathaiva putrāratnaṃ prabhūtaṃ tatha khādyabhojyam |na kiṃci dravyaṃ mi na
tyaktapūrvamima samādhiṃ pratikāṅkṣatā varam

smarāmi buddhāna sahasrakoṭiyotaduttare yattika gaṅgavālukāḥ |yehi sthihitvā iha gṛdhrakūṭeayaṃ
samādhirvaru śānta deśitaḥ

sarve ca śākyarṣabhanāmagheyāḥsarveṣu co rāhula nāma putrāḥ |ānandanāmā paricārakāścakapilāhvayāḥ
pravrajitāśca sarve

agreyugaṃ kolitaśāriputrāsamanāma sarve ca abhūṣi tāyinaḥ |samanāmikā co tada lokadhātuḥsarve'pi
cotpanna kaṣāyakāle

sarve mayā satkṛta te narendrāimāṃ carantena mi bodhicārikām |yāvanti co kāci jināna pūjāsarvā
kṛtā etu samādhimeṣatā

pratipattiya eṣa samādhi labhyatebahuprakārā pratipattiruktā |guṇeṣu sarveṣu pratiṣṭhitasyana
durlabhastasya samādhireṣaḥ

raseṣvagṛdhrasya alolupasyakuleṣvasaktasya anīrṣukasya |matrīvihārasya amatsarasyana durlabhastasya
samādhireṣaḥ

satkāralābheṣu anarthikasyaājīvaśuddhasya akiṃcanasya |viśuddhaśīlasya viśāradasyana durlabhastasya
samādhireṣaḥ

ārabdhavīryasya atandritasyaraṇyādhimuktasya dhute sthitasya |nairātmyakṣāntīya pratiṣṭhitasyana
durlabhastasya samādhireṣaḥ

sudāntacittasya anuddhatasyaīryāya caryāya pratiṣṭhitasya |tyāgādhimuktasya amatsarasyana durlabhastasya
samādhireṣaḥ

anuvyañjanalakṣaṇā buddhadharmāye'ṣṭādaśā kīrtita nāyakena |balāviśāradya na tasya durlabhādhāreti
yaḥ śāntamimaṃ samādhim

buddhena ye cakṣuṣa dṛṣṭa sattvā-sta ekakālasmi bhaveyu buddhāḥ |teṣaika ekasya bhaveyurāyuḥacintiyākalpasahasrakoṭiyaḥ

teṣaika ekasya śiro bhaveyuḥsarvu samudreṣu yathaiva vālukāḥ |yāvanti te sarva śiro bhaveyuḥśire
śire jihva bhaveyu tāttikāḥ

te tasya sarve bhaṇi ānuśaṃsāyo gātha dhāreyya itaḥ samādhitaḥ |na kiṃcimātraṃ parikīrtitaṃ
bhavetkiṃ vā punaryo hi śikṣitva dhāraye

dhūtān samādāya guṇāṃśca vartatespṛhenti devāsurayakṣaguhyakāḥ |rājāna bhonti anuyātru tasyadhāreti
yaḥ śānta samādhi durlabham

parigṛhīto bhavati jinebhi-rdevāśca nāgāḥ sada ānuyātrāḥ |pratyarthikāstasya śriya no sahantidhāreti
yaḥ śānta samādhi durlabham

anantu tasya pratibhānu bhotiananta sūtrāntasahasra bhāṣate |na tasya viṣṭhā nu kadāci bhotidhāreti
yaḥ śāntamimaṃ samādhim

drakṣyanti buddhamamitābhu nāyakaṃsukhāvatīṃ cāpyatha lokadhātum |ye paścime kāli mahābhayānakesamādhi
śrutvā imu dhārayeyuḥ

prakāśayitvā imu ānuśaṃsāadhyeṣate śāstu svayaṃ svayaṃbhūḥ |parinirvṛtasya mama paścikālesamādhi
dhāretha imaṃ viśuddham

ye keci buddhā daśasu diśāsuatītakāle'pi ca pratyutpannāḥ |sarve jinā atra samādhiśikṣitābudhyanti
bodhiṃ virajāmasaṃskṛtām

iti śrīsamādhirāje śālendrarāja(pūrvayoga)parivarto nāma dvitīyaḥ ||

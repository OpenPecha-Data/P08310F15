buddhānusmṛtiparivartaḥ |atha khalu candraprabhaḥ kumārabhūto bhagavantametadavocat-samādhiḥ
samādhiriti bhagavannucyate | katamasyaitaddharmasyādhivacanaṃ samādhiriti ? evamukte bhagavāṃścandraprabhaṃ
kumārabhūtametadavocat-samādhiḥ samādhiriti kumāra ucyate yaduta cittanidhyāptiḥ
| anupapattiḥ | apratisaṃdhiḥ | pratisaṃdhijñānam | apahṛtabhāratā | tathāgatajñānam
| buddhavṛṣabhitā | rāgacikitsā | doṣavyupaśamaḥ | mohasya prahāṇam | yuktayogitā |
ayuktavivarjanatā | akuśaladharmacchandaḥ | saṃsārānmokṣakāmatā | adhyāśayapratipattiḥ | jāgarikāyā
āsevanam | prahāṇasyānutsargaḥ | ārakṣā śukladharmāṇām | upapattiṣvaviśvāsaḥ | anabhisaṃskāraḥ
| karmaṇāmādhyātmikānāmāyatanānāmamanasikāraḥ | bāhyānāmāyatanānāmasamudācāraḥ
| ātmano'nutkarṣaṇam | pareṣāmapaṃsanatā | kuśaleṣvanadhyavasānam | pṛthagjaneṣvaviśvāsaḥ
|śīlasya niṣyandaḥ | durāsadatā | mahaujaskatā | ātmajñānam | acapalatā | īryāpathasaṃpadavasthānam
| avyāpādaḥ | apāruṣyam | pareṣvanutpīḍā | mitrāṇāmanurakṣaṇā | guhyamantrāṇāmārakṣaṇā
| avihiṃsā| śīlavatāmanutpīḍanā | ślakṣṇavacanatā | sarvatraidhātuke aniḥśritatā
| sarvadharmeṣu śūnyatā | ānulomikī kṣāntiḥ | sarvajñajñāne tīvracchandatā samādhiḥ samādhiriti
kumāra ucyate | yā eveṣvevaṃrūpeṣu dharmeṣu pratipattirapratipattiḥ, ayaṃ sa kumāra
ucyate samādhiriti ||atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ velāyāmimā gāthā abhāṣata-apāvṛtaṃ me amṛtasya
dvāramācakṣito dharmasvabhāvu yādṛśaḥ |nidarśitā me upapatti yādṛśīprakāśitā nirvṛti
sānuśaṃsā

vivarjanīyāḥ sada pāpamitrāḥkalyāṇamitrāśca niṣevitavyāḥ |vaneṣu vastavya gaṇān jahitvāmaitraṃ
ca cittaṃ sada bhāvanīyam

śuddhaṃ ca śīlaṃ sada rakṣitavyaṃdhūteṣu puṣṭiḥ sada vinditavyā |tyāgaśca prajñā ca niṣevitavyā
|na durlabho eṣa samādhi bheṣyati

tato labhitvā ima śāntabhūmiyasyāmabhūmiḥ pṛthu śrāvakāṇām |pratyakṣabhūtā sugatasya dharmapratilapsyathā
buddhaguṇānacintiyān

dṛṣṭvā narān bhājanabuddhimantāntān bodhicittasmi samādahetha |anuttare jñāni pratiṣṭhapitvāna
durlabho eṣa samādhirājaḥ

yasyārthi īrṣā puna saṃjaneyyāāhāri niṣyandiha pratyavekṣataḥ |paryeṣṭitaśco paribhogataścana
durlabho eṣa samādhi bheṣyate

samādhirājo yadi vaiṣa śūnyatoviśuddhaśīlānayu mūrdhni tiṣṭhati |svabhāvato dharma sadā samāhitābālā
na jānanti ayuktayogāḥ

yeṣāmayaṃ śānta samādhiriṣṭona teṣa jātu bhayabuddhi tiṣṭhati |sadānupaśyanti narāṇamuttama-mimāṃ
niṣevitva praśāntabhūmim

ākārato yaḥ smarate tathāgatānsa bhoti śāntendriyu śāntamānasaḥ |abhrāntacittaḥ satataṃ samāhitaḥśrutena
jñānena ca sāgaropamaḥ

asmin samādhau hi pratiṣṭhihittvāyaścaṃkrame caṃkrami bodhisattvaḥ |sa paśyati buddhasahasrakoṭiya-staduttare
yāttika gaṅgavālukāḥ

unmādu gaccheya narasya cittaṃyo buddhadharmāṇa pramāṇu gṛhṇīyāt |naivāpramāṇasya pramāṇamastiacintiyā
sarvaguṇehi nāyakāḥ

na so'sti sattvo daśasu diśāsuyo lokanāthena samaḥ kutottari |sarve hi sarvajñaguṇarupeta-mākāṅkṣatha
lapsyatha buddhajñānam

suvarṇavarṇena samucchrayeṇasamantaprāsādiku lokanāthaḥ |yasyātra ālambani cittu vartatesamāhitaḥ
socyati bodhisattvaḥ

asaṃskṛtaṃ saṃskṛtu jñātva vijñonimittasaṃjñāya vibhāvitāya |so ānimitte bhavati pratiṣṭhitaḥprajānatī
śūnyaka sarvadharmān

yo dharmakāye bhavati pratiṣṭhitoabhāva jānāti sa sarvabhāvān |abhāvasaṃjñāya vibhāvitāyana
rūpakāyena jinendra paśyati

ārocayāmi prativedayāmi voyathā yathā bahu ca vitarkayennaraḥ |tathā tathā bhavati tannimittacitta-stehi
vitarkehi tanniśritehi

evaṃ munīndraṃ smarato narasyaākārato jñānato aprameyataḥ |anusmṛtiṃ bhāvayataḥ sadā catannimnacittaṃ
bhavatī tatproṇam

sa caṃkramastho na niṣadyamāśritaākāṅkṣate puruṣavarasya jñānam |ākāṅkṣamāṇaḥ praṇigheti bodhayebhaviṣyahaṃ
loki niruttaro jinaḥ

sa buddha saṃjānati buddha paśyatebuddhāna co dharmata pratyavekṣate |iha samādhismi pratiṣṭhihitvānamasyate
buddha mahānubhāvān

kāyena vācā ca prasanna mānasābuddhāna varṇaṃ bhaṇatī abhīkṣṇam |tathāhi so bhāvitacittasaṃtatī
|rātriṃdivaṃ paśyati lokanāthān

yadāpi so bhoti gilāna āturaḥpravartate vedana māraṇāntikā |na buddhamārabhya smṛtiḥ pramuṣyatena
vedanābhiranusaṃharīyati

tathā hi tena vicinitva jñātāanāgatā āgata dharmaśūnyatā |so tādṛśe dharmanaye pratiṣṭhitona
khidyate citta carantu cārikām

tasmācchruṇitvā imu ānuśaṃsājanetha chandamatulāya bodhaye |mā paścakāle paritāpu bheṣyatasudurlabhaṃ
sugatavarāṇa darśanam

ahaṃ ca bhāṣeya praṇīta dharmaṃyūyaṃ ca śrutvāna samācarethāḥ |bhaiṣajya vastrāṃ ca gṛhītva
āturo-'panetu vyādhiṃ na prabhoti ātmanaḥ

tasmādvidhijñena vicakṣaṇenaimaṃ samādhiṃ pratikāṅkṣatā sadā |śīlaṃ śrutaṃ tyāgu niṣevitavyaṃna
durlabho eṣa samādhi bheṣyati

iti śrīsamādhirāje buddhānusmṛtiparivarto nāma caturthaḥ ||
